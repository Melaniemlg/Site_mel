[EN]

In 2021, after I graduated from the University of Clermont-Ferrand with a Master in children's book publishing, I decided to go on a brand new adventure: self-employment.

Strengthened by my previous experiences as an editor and foreign rights assistant for three children's publishing houses (Les petites bulles éditions, Les petites moustaches, Nathan jeunesse), I want for now on to work as a freelance. The services I propose are primarily addressed to publishers, but I am also willing to work directly with authors. I am looking for fiction books (with or without illustrations) around five big topics:

  • art
  • history
  • crime/investigation
  • fantasy
  • contemporary subjects (grief, sickness...)

Whether it is about reading, correcting, layout or representing manuscripts abroad, I truly desire to do as much as I can to fulfill your project!
